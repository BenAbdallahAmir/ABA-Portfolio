About Me

Behind A.B.A : A Creative Soul with a Coder’s Mindset

Hello! I’m Amir Ben Abdallah, better known as A.B.A — a freelance graphic designer and a software engineering student at EPI Digital School in Sousse. My journey began with a passion for visual storytelling, which naturally led me to explore the world of graphic design. Over the past few years, I’ve worked on a wide variety of freelance projects, designing everything from logos and posters to thumbnails, social media banners, and business cards. Each project has taught me how to communicate ideas visually, adapt to different client needs, and create meaningful, memorable designs. But I didn’t stop at the creative side. My curiosity about how things work led me to dive deeper into technology and software development. Pursuing a degree in software engineering has given me the tools to build functional, user-oriented digital solutions — websites, apps, and interfaces that are not only efficient but also thoughtfully designed. Whether it’s crafting visuals that stand out or developing digital solutions that perform, my mission is the same: to blend creativity and functionality into every project I take on. If you’re looking for a developer who understands both design and technology, and can transform your ideas into beautiful, efficient, and user-centered experiences, you’re in the right place. Let’s build something meaningful together.
